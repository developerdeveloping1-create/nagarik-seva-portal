Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I apply for Aadhaar or PAN Card?

Click on the Aadhaar or PAN Card card. A popup will confirm the official URL. Click "Go to Official Site" to open the government portal directly in a new tab.

Is NagarikSeva a government website?

No. NagarikSeva is an independent citizen-friendly platform. We are not affiliated with any government department. We simply provide direct links to official government portals to save your time.

Do I need to create an account?

No login or registration is required on NagarikSeva. You can directly browse and access any service. Accounts may be required on the official government websites themselves.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes — completely. NagarikSeva does not collect, store, or process any personal information. All your data goes directly to the official government portal.

Why am I redirected to another website?

All government services are officially processed only on government portals (.gov.in websites). We redirect you there because those are the only legitimate places to apply.

Can I access multiple services?

Yes! You can access any number of services at any time. Each click opens the respective official government website in a new tab so you can use many at once.
